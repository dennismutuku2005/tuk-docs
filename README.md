**tuk-docs** is a minimalist, ad-free document viewer designed for simplicity and speed. It provides a seamless experience for opening and managing your digital documents without the clutter of traditional apps.

### why tuk-docs?
- **Zero Ads**: Focus entirely on your reading with a 100% ad-free interface.
- **Multiple Formats**: View PDF, Word (.docx), and PowerPoint (.pptx) files with ease.
- **Smart Tools**: Quickly convert your photos or Word documents into PDF format.
- **Privacy Oriented**: Your documents stay on your device. We don't track or store your data.

### built with
